The enemies must be brought down to 0 HP. There are various strategies/optimizations that can be used here. An example of a full set of commands that works is: RaidNight Example Solution.xlsx

At certain milestones, a puzzle answer is given (within some flavor text):

  • Winning in cave entrance: BOING
  • Killing the dragon, or killing both of the other enemies, in sunken forest: BEERS
  • Winning in sunken forest: DEER
  • Reducing the dragon's health enough to trigger its ultimate, in ancient palace: SEMINAR (in reverse)
  • Winning in ancient palace: THE IMPURE
